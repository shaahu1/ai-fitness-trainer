Smart assistant for detecting body types and scheduling exercises

Our Research

In our research, we are trying to detect multiple human body types Apple, Inverted Triangle, Pear, Rectangle of Sri Lankan male using regular smartphone camera images with 2D images and use detected body type as a reference to suggest proper exercises for improving their physical appearance. VGG 16 machine learning model technology that we are hoping to use for this project.
With busy lifestyles and bad patterns of food usage the lack of exercise causes much trouble to humanity. The worst thing is people tend to do exercises by themselves and get into much more trouble or just waste their time without any guide to have proper exercise guide or what exercises to do to maximize their health and fitness goals. ICT can be used to make people much more aligned with fitness.

Objectives

Our main objective is to develop a mobile application to identify human body types based on machine learning, using our own collected dataset of 2D regular images of Sri Lankan males.

Specific Objective,

● To identify which human body parts are the most suitable for detecting the overall body type of humans using medical science.
● To identify which machine learning algorithms are the most suitable for detecting human body types.
● To develop an android app which assists people with guidance for the exercises and suggest meal plans according to their body type.
