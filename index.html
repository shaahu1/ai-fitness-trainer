Smart assistant for detecting body types and scheduling exercises

Our Research

In our research, we are trying to detect multiple human body types Apple, Inverted Triangle, Pear, Rectangle of Sri Lankan male using regular smartphone camera images with 2D images and use detected body type as a reference to suggest proper exercises for improving their physical appearance. VGG 16 machine learning model technology that we are hoping to use for this project.
With busy lifestyles and bad patterns of food usage the lack of exercise causes much trouble to humanity. The worst thing is people tend to do exercises by themselves and get into much more trouble or just waste their time without any guide to have proper exercise guide or what exercises to do to maximize their health and fitness goals. ICT can be used to make people much more aligned with fitness.

Objectives

Our main objective is to develop a mobile application to identify human body types based on machine learning, using our own collected dataset of 2D regular images of Sri Lankan males.

Specific Objective,

● To identify which human body parts are the most suitable for detecting the overall body type of humans using medical science.
● To identify which machine learning algorithms are the most suitable for detecting human body types.
● To develop an android app which assists people with guidance for the exercises and suggest meal plans according to their body type.

Works

Data Set

The dataset plays a significant role in the performance of any machine learning-based system. For that project, we need to collect human body images for the dataset. Due to ethical and social concerns, we decided to collect and build our own dataset by manually taking images and browsing images through the internet. The pose of the human body is much more critical to detect the overall body type hence data collection process became slower, We advised volunteers to make a T-pose or stand straight spine erect and hand beside the body and further from their body. Finally, we collected 530 male body images in Sri Lankan and Asian regions.

ML Model

We tried to train a model with CNN and train 2 pre-trained models ResNet50 and VGG16. In terms of accuracy, we chose the VGG16 model to train our model. Finally, we got the model with 82% accuracy and for that, we use data augmentation techniques. All model training is done by google collabs.

Used tools : VGG16 and Google Collab

Mobile Application Development

We selected the android platform as the base Operating system for which our application is to be installed and we have used Figma online web application to catch the User Interfaces of the application. We used the android studio as the IDE and the language is java. To store the user, exercises, and meal plan data we use firebase real-time database. The ML Model was stored locally in the application.

Used tools : Android Studio and Firebase
